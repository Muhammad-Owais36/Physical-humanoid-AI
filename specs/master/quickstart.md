# Quickstart Guide: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book

## Overview

This quickstart guide provides a brief introduction to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics documentation book. The book is structured around four core modules that build upon each other to provide comprehensive knowledge of AI systems in the physical world.

The book follows a modular approach with four interconnected modules:

1. **The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)** - Middleware for robot control
2. **The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)** - Physics simulation and environment building
