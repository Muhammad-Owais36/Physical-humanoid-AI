# APA Citation Guide for AI-Robot Brain Research Paper

## Journal Article Citation Format

**Basic Format:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, *volume*(issue), pages. DOI or URL

**Example:**
Smith, J. D., & Johnson, M. K. (2023). Advanced perception systems in humanoid robotics. *IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters*, *8*(3), 1234-1241. https://doi.org/10.1109/LRA.2023.1234567

## Technical Documentation Citation Format

**Basic Format:**
Company/Organization Name. (Year). Title of documentation. URL

**Example:**
NVIDIA Corporation. (2024). NVIDIA Isaac ROS documentation. https://docs.nvidia.com/isaac-ros/

## Conference Paper Citation Format

**Basic Format:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year, Month). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of Conference Name* (pp. page-page). Publisher. DOI or URL

**Example:**
Brown, L. M., & Davis, R. T. (2023, May). Hardware-accelerated VSLAM for humanoid navigation. In *Proceedings of International Conference on Robotics and Automation* (pp. 567-574). IEEE. https://doi.org/10.1109/ICRA.2023.1234567

## Book Citation Format

**Basic Format:**
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of book* (Edition). Publisher. DOI or URL

## Chapter in Edited Book Citation Format

**Basic Format:**
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In A. Editor & B. Editor (Eds.), *Title of book* (pp. page-page). Publisher.

## Common Reference Types for This Paper

### Research Papers on NVIDIA Isaac:
- Author, A. A., et al. (Year). Title of Isaac-related research. *Journal Name*, *volume*(issue), pages. DOI

### Technical Documentation:
- NVIDIA Corporation. (Year). Isaac Sim user guide. https://docs.nvidia.com/isaac-sim/

### Conference Proceedings:
- Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of paper on humanoid robotics. In *Proceedings of Conference* (pages). Publisher.

## In-Text Citation Format

For paraphrased information:
- Single author: (Author, Year) or Author (Year) found that...
- Two authors: (Author & Author, Year) or Author and Author (Year) demonstrated...
- Three or more authors: (Author et al., Year) or Author et al. (Year) showed...

For direct quotes:
- Include page number: (Author, Year, p. #) or Author (Year) stated "...quote" (p. #)

## Notes
- Always use the most specific publication date available (Year, Year, Month, or Year, Month, Day)
- For online sources without DOI, provide the full URL
- For sources with multiple authors, list up to 20 authors; for more than 21, list first 19, then ellipsis (...), then final author
- Italicize journal names, volume numbers, and book titles
